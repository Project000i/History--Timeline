Kyrgyz Influencers

Atake-baatyr

Atake-baatyr Atake-baatyr - Kyrgyz statesman, politician and military leader. One of Kyrgyz leaders in wars with Dzungars and Kazakhs. Organiser of the first Kyrgyz embassy in Russia.
Year and place of birth: 1737, Andijan region (modern Uzbekistan).
Year and place of death: 1787, Issyk-Kul Basin (Modern Kyrgyzstan). Atake-baatyr is known for being the first Kyrgyz ruler to send delegations to the Russian Empire in 1785-1789. During his diplomatic missions, he established friendly relations with Russia, as well as a new trade route from Russia to Asia, and gained protection for his family from external enemies

Kurmanjan-datka

Kurmanjan-datka was a Kyrgyz stateswoman and military leader and ruler of the Alai Kyrgyz from 1832 to 1876. Also known as the "Queen of Alai", "Queen of the South" and "Mother of the Nation".
Year and place of birth: 22 March 1811, Alai Mountains (present-day Kyrgyzstan)
Year and place of death: 1907, Fergana province (modern Kyrgyzstan).
Interesting fact: The first denomination with the image of Kurmanjan-datka was issued in 1994, the most common banknote was issued in 2016, and in 2024 a new denomination of 50 soms was shown, also with the image of Kurmanjan-datka. Having taken over the power and title of "Datka" from her husband Alymbek Datka, Kurmanjan Datka had to replace her husband by taking power into her own hands.For the sake of her people, she had to accept the execution of her youngest son Kamchybek in order to preserve the future of the Alai Kyrgyz.She became the first and only woman in the history of the Muslim world to be given such a high status.
